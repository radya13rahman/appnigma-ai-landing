Questions

Do I need Salesforce development skills or coding knowledge?

No. Appnigma is designed for product teams and founders, not Salesforce developers. You describe what you want to build in plain English — Appnigma handles all the technical implementation.

How quickly can an integration go live?

Most customers ship their first managed package within 14 days. Some go live even faster — within a week — depending on the complexity of the integration.

Does Appnigma help with getting listed on the AppExchange?

Yes. Every package Appnigma generates is built to pass Salesforce Security Review and meet AppExchange listing requirements out of the box.

Can I deploy across multiple Salesforce orgs?

Yes. As a managed package, your integration can be installed across unlimited customer orgs — just like any other AppExchange product.

Can I update or maintain integrations after deployment?

Absolutely. You own the generated code and can update or extend your package at any time using Appnigma or directly in the Salesforce dev tools.

Is the platform secure / enterprise-ready?

Yes. Security is core to the product. Every generated artifact follows Salesforce Security Review best practices with 0 PMD violations.

How does this compare to traditional Salesforce consulting?

Traditional consulting costs $150K+ per year, takes 6+ months, and you don't own the output. Appnigma ships in days, costs a fraction of the price, and you own all the code.

What kinds of prompts work for building integrations?

Plain English descriptions work best. e.g. "Build a Salesforce managed package that syncs contacts from our app, creates an Opportunity when they upgrade, and shows a Lightning component on the Account page."
